San Diego Housing

Posted on Sat 02 June 2018 in Finance

Since we are moving to San Diego in July, figured would play around with Zillow data to check out the market in our zip code.

Some metrics that I pulled:

Inventory Seasonally Adjusted: I pulled inventory both for greater SD and our zip code. Both show the story of rapidly shrinking inventory

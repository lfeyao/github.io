2017 Goals and 2016 Review

Posted on Wed 04 January 2017 in Random

My 3 Goals for 2017:

  1. Keep better in touch with family/friends. After leaving NY and moving to LA and now CHI, I realized how the circle of people that I actually keep in touch with keeps getting smaller and smaller. I will make more of an effort to reach out.

  2. Learn more about crypto. I've invested some amount in bitcoin but haven't spent much time with the protocol or other projects like ethereum. I plan to take the Princeton Coursera course and look into this area more.

  3. Write more. Simply a repeat since I didn't write much in 2016.

Review of my 3 Goals for 2016:

  1. Cook more. This was largely accomplished in 2016. I now have a rudimentary knowledge of what to buy at the grocery store (always keep garlic, ginger, green onion, and some Chinese vegetables on hand!) and what to put together to create a quick dish. One of my favorite books on cooking was The Food Lab. It was very scientific in its approach and helped me appreciate basic principles. Whereas before I would always just turn the heat to max setting, now I have a greater appreciation for heat at lower temperatures and different tools to use.

  2. Learn more about the pharmaceutical industry and improve my Chinese. This was somewhat accomplished. I spent a fair amount of time learning about the pharma industry but eventually baby duties took over. Same thing with Chinese, checked out a bunch of vocab books but never managed to get through them all.

  3. Write more. This was a failure. I didn't block off time to write and this blog went ignored.
